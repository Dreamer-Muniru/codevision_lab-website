Our Mission

Inspiring the Next Generation of Coders

We're reimagining STEM education and workforce development to empower every student with the technical skills necessary for the jobs of the future.

We focus on skill building and workforce development in technologies that are going to change the world over the next decade, such as Quantum Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and Big Data.

With a commitment to accessible, supportive computer science education, we offer learning pathways and programs for individuals at every stage of their STEM journey, including:

K-12 students and educators
Community college and university students and faculty
Members of the workforce
